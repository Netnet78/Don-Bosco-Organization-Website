Unlocking Futures: Education for All

Don Bosco Poipet is a non-profit organization dedicated to breaking the cycle of poverty by providing underprivileged children and young people with the opportunity to access quality education. Our mission is simple yet profound: to empower those in need with the transformative gift of schooling. Click below to learn more about how we're making education a reality for every child and youth in our community.

Empowering Young Lives Through Education

At Don Bosco Mission in Poipet, we are dedicated to nurturing the potential of children and young people through education. Our mission is to provide them with the utmost care, ensuring their learning and living environments foster holistic well-being, both physically and mentally. We particularly focus on street children and those at risk, aiming to offer them opportunities that will positively shape their futures. Join us in making a difference, one young life at a time.

About Us

Don Bosco Poipet is a branch of Don Bosco Foundation of Cambodia “DBFC” who arrived in Cambodia since 1991 and currently registered under various ministries as both Local and International NGO. Our mission since then and now are helping the poor children and young people through education in various level from Kindergarten till high school as well as technical training.

Don Bosco Schools has presented in 132 countries around the world and known as SALESIAN OF DON BOSCO. Visit: db.org for more information.

Our mission at Don Bosco Poipet is started in 2002 with ambition to bring back especially street children at this small town next to the boarder of Cambodia-Thailand to go to school. Currently, we are implementing various programs such as scholarship program known as children fund, boarder for poor students, educational program from grade 1 to 12 as well as technical high school in Electrical and Computer Skill. Around 500 students benefit from these programs each year.
